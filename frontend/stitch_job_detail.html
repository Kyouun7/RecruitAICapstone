About the Role

As a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at RecruitAI Labs, you will lead the evolution of our proprietary talent-matching algorithms. You will work within a cross-functional team of researchers and product engineers to design, build, and deploy large-scale machine learning systems. This role demands a deep understanding of Transformer architectures and LLM orchestration to power our next generation of recruiter tools.
